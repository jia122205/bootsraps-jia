About SMCC

SMCC (Sample Multi-Community College) is dedicated to providing quality education and training to our students. Our mission is to empower individuals through knowledge and skills.

We offer a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of our diverse student body, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to succeed in their chosen field.

Vision

Saint Michael envisions becoming a University by 2035, upholding spiritual formation and excellence in teaching, service, and research.

Mission

SMCC shall provide spiritual formation and a learning culture that will ensure students receive an excellent and rewarding learning experience that transforms lives, enhances spirituality, develops skills, and prepares future leaders.

SMCC shall engage in dynamic, innovative, and interdisciplinary research to advance institutional initiatives.

SMCC shall commit to serving diverse local communities by fostering innovation through service-learning that enhances reciprocal community partnerships for spiritual and social development.

Goals

Uphold a culture of excellence in spiritual formation, instruction, research, and extension, thus producing graduates who are globally competent, spiritually embodied, and socially responsible.
